TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

Q: Files show as RED in preview?
A: Close the files in other programs (video editors, image viewers, etc.)

Q: Preview shows "Error: Base name cannot be empty"?
A: Enter a name in the "Base Name" field

Q: Preview shows "Contains forbidden characters"?
A: Don't use these characters: < > : " / \ | ? *

Q: Some files weren't renamed?
A: Check the results dialog - it will show which files failed and why

Q: Numbers aren't in the right order?
A: Change "Sort By" option (try "Date Created" or "Alphabetical")
